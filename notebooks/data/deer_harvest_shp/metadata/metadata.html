Section 1 Overview
Originator Minnesota Department of Natural Resources (DNR)
Title Minnesota Deer Harvest
Abstract Annual deer harvest. Number of deer taken per kill block, summed by age and method of harvest (firearm, archery, muzzle loader)
Purpose To summarize and map deer harvest information by weapon used and antlered status: buck or antlerless.
